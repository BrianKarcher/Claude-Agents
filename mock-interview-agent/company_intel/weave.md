# Weave Communications — Interview Intel

**Location:** Lehi, Utah  
**Product:** Customer communication platform for SMBs (phones, messaging, scheduling)  
**Stack:** Go (Golang) prominent; cloud-based SaaS

## Interview Process

1. Recruiter phone screen (~15–30 min)
2. Short HackerRank coding challenge (30 min, via Google Meet)
3. Technical interviews with team members (coding + system design)
4. Behavioral interviews
5. Culture fit call with CEO or leadership

**Total rounds:** ~3–4  
**Timeline:** 2–4 weeks average  
**Difficulty:** 3/5 — moderate, solid fundamentals expected

## Coding

- HackerRank link sent during a Google Meet session
- Golang knowledge tested
- Standard DSA problems; not known for extreme difficulty
- Expect: arrays, strings, graphs, trees — NeetCode 150 / Blind 75 tier

## System Design

- SMB-scale systems (not hyperscale)
- Focus areas: communication platforms, scheduling, messaging, multi-tenant SaaS
- Practical tradeoffs matter more than theoretical perfection

## Behavioral

- STAR format expected
- Key themes:
  - Explaining technical problems to non-technical stakeholders
  - Handling challenging situations / conflict
  - Culture fit (startup mentality, ownership, adaptability)
- CEO culture fit round — values alignment is important to them

## Notes

- Post-onsite take-home assignment possible (submitted within 24 hours)
- 75% positive interview experience rating on Glassdoor
- Company values communication skills highly — both technical and interpersonal
