## Methodology

Live prices fetched 2026-05-06 13:00–14:30 ET via Google Finance / Yahoo Finance / aggregator data. Each candidate scored on the 5-pillar 0–2 rubric:
- A = Revenue growth (forward 12 months)
- B = FCF margin (SBC-adjusted where material)
- C = Valuation (P/S, P/FCF, P/E in context of growth)
- D = Moat (durability and width)
- E = Risk (regulatory, execution, competitive, balance sheet)

Score is the sum, max 10. The rubric is identical to 2026-04-14 to enable comparability.
