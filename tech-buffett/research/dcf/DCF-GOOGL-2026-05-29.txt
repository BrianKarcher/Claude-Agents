1. MODEL ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

PRIMARY UNDERWRITING COLUMN:   SBC-adjusted Free Cash Flow
                                = OCF - Capex - SBC
                                = FCF - SBC

WHY SBC-ADJ FCF (not GAAP EPS, not reported FCF):
  - SBC is a real cost to existing shareholders (dilution + cash repurchase
    offset)
  - Reported FCF ignores SBC; GAAP earnings include it but are obscured by
    one-time equity-mark volatility ($36.9B Q1'26 alone)
  - SBC-adj FCF is the cleanest cash-on-cash measure of value created for
    long-term equityholders

DISCOUNT RATES:
  - 9%  (WIDE moat, net cash, AAA-equivalent balance sheet)
  - 11% (antitrust uncertainty premium + capex-cycle execution premium)
  - 13% (stress / bear-case haircut)

  The 10% hurdle referenced in the gate is interpolated linearly between 9%
  and 11% results for sensitivity-table purposes.

TERMINAL VALUE METHODS (both run; equal-weighted averaged):
  - Perpetuity-growth:   3.0% terminal g in all scenarios
                         TV = FCF_FY36 * (1+g) / (WACC - g)
  - Exit multiple:       Bear 18x, Base 22x, Bull 25x SBC-adj FCF FY35

SHARE COUNT:
  Static-share IV computed first; buyback overlay treated as a kicker per
  scenario. Equity-side IV reported per current share count of ~12,100M;
  scenario-specific share count drift incorporated in the per-share TV.

NET CASH & WAYMO/OTHER BETS SOTP:
  - Start: $49.3B net cash at Q1'26 ($4.07/share)
  - Net cash evolution modeled per scenario (sec 7)
  - Waymo SOTP added as scenario-level EV kicker (sec 8 of marching orders;
    Bear $0 / Base $60B / Bull $200B)

CONSOLIDATED EBIT:
  Built bottom-up: Services rev x Services OM + Cloud rev x Cloud OM + Other
  Bets EBIT loss + Alphabet-level overhead drag. Antitrust haircut layered
  on top starting FY28.

NORMALIZED OI&E:
  Q1'26 had $36.9B equity-securities gain. Normalized OI&E set to $5B/yr
  base case (interest income on cash holdings ~3-4%) and 0 in bear/bull
  for simplicity (volatility cancels over long horizons).

TAX:
  18% effective rate (FY25 was 16.8%; conservative uptick reflects the
  post-2025 US tax law transition + likely OECD minimum tax impacts in
  outer years; deep dive used 18% as central case).

4. SBC, CAPEX, AND SBC-ADJUSTED FREE CASH FLOW
================================================================================

OPERATING CASH FLOW BRIDGE (simplified):
  Approx OCF = EBIT * (1 - tax rate) + D&A - Working capital
  We assume D&A ≈ depreciation of installed property + intangible amort.
  Since capex peaks at ~$148B+ in FY26, D&A ramps materially through FY30
  and beyond. For tractability, we approximate:
    OCF ≈ EBIT * (1 - tax) + D&A
    with D&A modeled separately (sec 4.2 below).

  Cleaner alternative: build SBC-adjusted FCF directly from
    SBC-adj FCF = NOPAT + D&A - Capex - SBC - delta-WC
  where NOPAT = EBIT * (1-t).

  For simplicity, since working capital changes are small relative to scale,
  we model: SBC-adj FCF ≈ NOPAT + D&A - Capex - SBC.
