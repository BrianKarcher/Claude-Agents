## OpenAI Partnership Update

- New definitive agreement signed Oct 2025; OpenAI PBC formed and recapitalized
- MSFT holds ~27% of OpenAI on as-converted diluted basis (equity method, HLBV)
- Total funding commitments: $13B, of which $11.7B funded as of Dec 31, 2025
- OpenAI contracted to purchase $250B incremental Azure services
- MSFT no longer has right of first refusal on OpenAI compute
- Dilution gain recognized in Q2 from recapitalization (part of $9.97B other income)

## Notable Items / One-Time

- **Other income (expense), net:** $9.97B in Q2 FY2026 (vs $(2.29B) PY) -- driven by OpenAI dilution gain and investment revaluation; this is a non-recurring item
- **PP&E, net:** Surged to $261.1B from $205.0B in 6 months (+$56.2B, +27%) -- unprecedented datacenter buildout pace
- **H1 capex:** $49.3B (annualized ~$98.5B pace vs $64.6B FY2025) -- significant ramp
- **D&A:** $17.3B H1 (annualized ~$34.7B, roughly matching FY2025's full-year $34.2B already in first half)
- **Gaming impairment charges:** Included in More Personal Computing operating expenses
- **Server component receivables:** $15.1B in other current assets -- MSFT pre-purchasing GPU/server components at scale
- No restatements or material accounting changes
