## Notable Items

- **OpenAI new agreement (Oct 28, 2025 subsequent event):** Extended partnership; OpenAI contracted $250B incremental Azure services; MSFT no longer has right of first refusal on compute; MSFT holds ~27% of OpenAI PBC on as-converted diluted basis
- **PP&E, net:** Rose from $205.0B to $230.9B in one quarter (+$25.9B) -- massive datacenter buildout continues
- **D&A:** $13.1B in Q1 vs $7.4B PY -- 77% increase reflects accelerated infra depreciation
- **Other receivables (server components):** Not broken out in Q1 (was $8.2B at Jun 30, 2025; see Q2 for $15.1B)
- **Unearned revenue (ST):** Declined to $59.0B from $64.6B (seasonal Q1 drawdown)
- FX favorable 2% across revenue, GM, OI
