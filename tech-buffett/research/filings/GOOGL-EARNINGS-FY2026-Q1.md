## Pichai Commentary (Highlights)

- **Backlog:** Google Cloud backlog **nearly doubled QoQ to over $460 billion** — single most important data point in the release.
- **Search AI:** Search had a "strong quarter with AI experiences driving usage, queries at an all-time high"
- **Consumer AI:** Strongest-ever quarter for consumer AI plans (Gemini App)
- **Paid subscriptions:** 350M total paid subscribers (YouTube + Google One)
- **Gemini Enterprise:** +40% QoQ growth in paid monthly active users
- **Gemini API:** First-party models processing >16B tokens per minute via direct API (+60% QoQ)
- **Waymo:** Surpassed 500,000 fully autonomous rides per week

## Capital Actions

- **Notes issued:** $31.1B net proceeds in Q1 2026 (general corporate purposes; partial M&A funding)
- **Wiz acquisition close:** Reflected in Q1 (goodwill +$24.4B; $33.6B acquisitions, net of cash)
- **Buybacks:** $0 in Q1 2026 (notable pause — first zero-buyback quarter in years; likely M&A-funding posture)
- **Dividend:** Quarterly dividend raised 5% from $0.21 to $0.22 effective June 2026 payment
- **Capex:** $35.7B Q1 alone — annualized run-rate **>$140B** (vs. $91.4B FY25); FY26 capex on pace to roughly **double** FY25

## One-time / Non-recurring / Accounting Items

- **$36.9B unrealized gain on equity securities** (primarily non-marketable; +$28.7B net of tax = +$2.35 EPS). This is **volatile and non-operating** — must be removed for normalized earnings.
- **Senior notes issuance:** $31.1B net proceeds (long-term debt step-up)
- **Wiz close:** Goodwill +$24.4B, intangibles +$8.2B, non-marketable securities +$38.3B
- **No Q1 buyback** — a sequencing decision around M&A close, not a permanent shift

## Guidance

No formal forward revenue or EPS guidance issued in the release. Forward-looking commentary:
- Significant capex increase vs. FY25 reaffirmed (already trending to ~$140B annualized)
- Continued investment in technical infrastructure for AI
