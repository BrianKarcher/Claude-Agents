### Red flags vs. thesis

1. **Overall NDR fell to 110%** and management guided **NDR to slightly decline by year-end**. They attribute this to lapping the 2024 pricing actions (+12% contribution that fades in Q2'26). The deep dive priced in NDR drift; the print confirms structural pricing-action fade.
2. **Adjusted FCF margin compressed to 29% from 39%** YoY. Drivers: $20M buyback-related interest income loss + FX + AI compute cost. FY26 FCF margin guide of 19-20% is well below FY25's 26%. Capital allocation cost.
3. **Q2 guide growth +18-19%** is below Q1 actual +24% — implying meaningful deceleration through the year (organic moderation as 2024 pricing action laps out + lower-end SMB top-of-funnel softness Zinman called out).
4. **Gross margin slipped from 90% to 89%** — Eliran flagged expected drift to mid-80s as AI compute costs ramp. Watch line for FY27.
5. **Enterprise customers signing multi-year deals are reducing 12-month NDR for >$100K cohort** — this is the right kind of "problem" (longer commitments) but reduces the headline NDR metric vs. prior runs.

### Green flags vs. thesis

1. **Revenue beat AND guide raise** — a clean print on the most-watched line. FY26 raised to +19-20% from +18-19%.
2. **$500K customers +74% YoY** is the strongest expansion signal and exceeds the deep-dive bar (+50%) materially.
3. **Multi-product attach hit 34% (Q4: 29%)** — fastest progression in MNDY's history toward the strategic 35%+ target.
4. **GAAP operating income doubled to $19.8M** — record. GAAP profitability building.
5. **Net new ARR ~10% from AI products** — far higher than initiation/deep dive penciled in (deep dive: "AI agents at 60% prob of $100M ARR by 2028"). This is a positive surprise on the optionality axis.
6. **$553M deployed in buyback in Q1 at ~$76 average** — capital-allocation velocity at the upper end of deep-dive expectations. Float retired ~14% in a single quarter is **highly material**.
7. **Headcount flat from here for the rest of FY26** — operating leverage thesis explicit from CFO.
8. **OneAI acquisition** — adds voice agents to the AI Work Platform stack; thoughtful tuck-in.
9. **Gross retention at "historical highs"** — sticky-product moat strengthening.
10. **RPO +33% to $880M** is the lagging-into-leading indicator that disagrees with the headline deceleration concern. Booked future revenue is accelerating.

### Buy assessment

The Q1 print is a **clean upside surprise vs. the deep-dive gate framework**: all 5 primary buy gates pass, the FY26 guide was raised, the buyback velocity is at the high end of deep-dive modeling, and the AI net-new-ARR signal is materially above plan. The NDR compression to 110% was expected (pricing-action lap) and the FCF margin compression to 19-20% was modeled. **No thesis-break triggers fired.**
