## Antitrust / Regulatory Status (as of 10-K filing)

- **DOJ Search case:** Final judgment Dec 2025 imposes restrictions on distribution and requires data-sharing/syndication. Alphabet appealed Jan 2026; DOJ also appealed Feb 2026. Remedies stayed pending appeal.
- **DOJ Ad Tech case:** April 2025 ruling — publisher tools found anticompetitive; advertiser tools and DoubleClick/AdMeld acquisitions cleared. Remedy phase Sept-Nov 2025; final judgment pending. Alphabet plans to appeal.
- **EC fines (cumulative historical):** €2.4B (Shopping, 2017), €4.3B (Android, 2018), €1.5B (AdSense, 2019); $3.5B EC fine accrued FY25.
- **Texas AG Ad Tech case:** Trial follows DOJ remedies decision.
