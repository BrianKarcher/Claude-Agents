### Net read on deceleration drivers

Green's macro commentary frames the +12% growth as **cyclical/macro-driven, not structural**:
- Geopolitics, tariffs, CPG/Auto category-specific softness
- Supply glut (sellers' over-supply) is a buyer's-market tailwind for TTD long-term
- International is growing faster — diversification working
- JBP signings +55% and new JBP spend +40% YoY are **leading indicators that disagree with the lagging revenue print**

This is consistent with the deep dive's framing that deceleration is ~60% cyclical / ~40% structural. The Q1 print does not invalidate the deceleration call; if anything, it adds more cyclical signal (macro/tariff/CPG specifics).
