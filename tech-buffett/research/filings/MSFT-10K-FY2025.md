## Other Key Items

- **Stock-based compensation:** $11,974M (4.2% of revenue)
- **Unrecognized SBC costs:** $21.6B as of Jun 30, 2025 (3-year weighted avg vesting)
- **RPO (remaining performance obligations):** $375B total, $368B commercial (as of Jun 30, 2025), ~40% recognized in next 12 months
- **Shares outstanding:** 7,434M (Jun 30, 2025); 7,433M (Jul 24, 2025)
- **Dividends per share (quarterly):** $0.75 - $0.83 range during FY2025
- **OpenAI partnership:** $13B total funding commitments; OpenAI API exclusive to Azure; MSFT right of first refusal on OpenAI compute (note: subsequently relinquished per new Oct 2025 agreement)
- **Activision Blizzard:** Closed FY2024; contributed to Gaming revenue growth
- **Segment recast in FY2025:** Commercial components of M365 moved to Productivity & Business Processes segment

## One-Time Items / Notable

- Other expense, net of $(4,901)M in FY2025 vs $(1,646)M in FY2024 -- includes OpenAI investment losses
- Property & equipment, net surged from $135.6B to $205.0B (+51%) reflecting massive datacenter buildout
- D&A rose from $22.3B to $34.2B (+53%) -- reflects accelerated datacenter depreciation
- No restatements or accounting changes noted
