## Segment / Channel / Geography (Q1 2026 YoY)

- **CTV:** Americas +13% YoY (~80% of total CTV revenue); global CTV ex-legacy DSP +18%.
- **Mobile app:** +25%+ YoY.
- **Display:** +5% YoY.
- **Emerging revenues** (Activate, Commerce Media, Connect, AI Solutions): +80%+ YoY, ~14% of total revenue.
- **Activate direct buy:** more than tripled (>3x) YoY.
- **Mid-market DSP ad spend:** +20%+ YoY (added 50+ DSPs in past year).
- **Geography:** APAC +25%, EMEA +10%, Americas (12)% (legacy DSP drag).
- **Impressions processed:** 94.2T (+26% YoY); now >1 trillion impressions/day.
- **Cost of revenue per million impressions:** down 20% on TTM basis.
- **SPO:** >56% of total platform activity.

## AI / AgenticOS (thesis-relevant)

- AgenticOS launched Jan 2026 (CES). >1,000 AI-powered deals transacted to date; 20+ operational AI agents; 30+ live fully autonomous end-to-end campaigns globally (US, France, Netherlands, Australia, India).
- Every advertiser that ran an agentic campaign returned to run more.
- 80–90% time savings in campaign setup; agencies cited: Butler/Till, MiQ, Breakthrough, Amnet, Abovo Maxlead, Horizon Media.
- Activate + AgenticOS: 30–40% CPM improvements, 40% more working media; full dollar stays in PubMatic ecosystem (incremental SSP rev share on top of Activate rev share).
- Pricing: Activate rev-share based; exploring subscription models for some AI tools.
- Owned NVIDIA GPU infrastructure (Triton Inference Server) cited as structural cost/latency advantage vs. cloud-based competitors.

## Strategic / Partnership Highlights

- **Amazon DSP Dynamic Traffic Engine (DTE):** integrated globally; up to 10% CPM lift for publishers.
- **Walmart Connect Select:** preferred supply partner for CTV (first-party shopper data).
- **PayPal Ads ID:** 25B+ transactions, 400M verified PayPal/Venmo accounts; deterministic identity.
- **Unity LevelPlay** integrated into OpenWrap SDK; now live with all 3 leading mobile mediation platforms (AppLovin MAX, Google AdMob, Unity LevelPlay) = >90% global SDK inventory. Zynga integrated SDK.
- **AdRoll:** first DSP to connect via Model Context Protocol (MCP) for autonomous PMP deal troubleshooting.
- Live sports / FIFA World Cup: expects >100M high-value impressions/day; 28 of top 30 global streamers on platform.

## Google Antitrust Optionality (CEO commentary)

- Google ~60% ad-tech market share; PubMatic ~4%. Each 1% of share captured ≈ $50M–$75M revenue at ~80% margin. CEO sees opportunity to "more than double" PubMatic share post-remedy. Verdict on remedies expected imminently; behavioral remedies could be implemented quickly.

## Leadership / Capital Allocation

- **Leadership transitions:** Chief Growth Officer (Pauline, 15 yrs, health reasons) and Americas CRO (Kyle Dozeman, 13 yrs, entrepreneurship) both departing for personal reasons; both in transition roles. Global CRO search underway (Heidrick & Struggles); intent to consolidate revenue functions under one global CRO. Expected hire within "a couple of months."
- **Buybacks:** repurchased 1.0M Class A shares in Q1 (2.1% of fully diluted shares); $8.5M cash deployed. Cumulative since Feb 2023: 13.5M shares for $190M; $85M remaining authorized through 2026.
- 40th consecutive quarter of positive adjusted EBITDA.
- Since 2021 through Q1 2026: >$429M cumulative OCF, >$232M cumulative FCF.
